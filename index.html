About Karaikudi

Karaikudi is a prominent town in Tamil Nadu, known for its rich heritage, unique architecture, and vibrant culture. Located in the Sivaganga district, it is the cultural capital of the Chettinad region.

Tradition and Culture

The traditions of Karaikudi are deeply rooted in the Chettinad culture, which is famous for its grand architecture, flavorful cuisine, and exquisite crafts. The Chettinad mansions, with their intricate designs and opulent interiors, are a testament to the region's rich history.
